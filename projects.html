Rental Car Management System

A Java-based project to track car rentals, returns, and mileage-based charges. It is a simple system where administrators can add new cars to inventory, assign cars to users, track when vehicles are returned, and calculate rental charges based on miles driven.

The system uses object-oriented programming principles such as classes, inheritance, and encapsulation.

Key features:

  • Add, remove, and update car inventory.
  • Track rentals and returns by customers.
  • Calculate charges based on mileage.

Technologies: Java, BlueJ
